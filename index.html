Digital

There is nothing on the page.

How can we solve tangible problems around our own community if we choose to stay placeless and disconnected in the digital realm.

as I build this website I think of how little of my own personality is imbued into this interaction. when I crinkle a page, is it rough? do I fold it back gently? Do I rip the page out? There are no constraints beyond the binding of the book. Digital gives you little choice - ah. this is what it looks like when you hover over a link. I guess you can choose how quickly and smoothly you scroll down a page but there is so little variability. your computer has your mouse set on a certain speed you scroll set to a certain speed, and if you must ask the computer.

Digital cramps my style.

Digital is placeless.

Digital is trendy.

In digital you can be specific but you have to work so hard to create a semblance of variability and randomness. Everythign is geared towards organization. Maybe, maybe then the answer is not in going just back to physical, but embracing
