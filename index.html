A Comparative Study of User Experience between Physical Objects and their Digital Surrogates

There exists four dimensions to an interactions affects on user experience and their corresponding attributes

Sensual

Definition

The visual and sensorial aspects ot he physical object through touch and can include all five senses

Attributes

Aesthetics attributes

Affect through visual and tangible experience (manipulation)

Novelty & curiosity

Unique features and unusual or novel qualities

Emotional

Definition

An emotional effect a user has with the objects

Attributes

Inquisitive & Interpretive

To provoke analytical thinking or to reflect upon.

Emotions & Feelings

Real and or imagined emotions (joy, fear, sadness) or feelings (state of mind) such as satisfaction or contentment

Spatiotemporal

Definition

Transporting the user into a specific time or place in history

Attributes

Connectionss

Making connections through memory of experiences. Creating a story or narrative

Numinous

Definition

Arousing an epiphanic experience between the user and the object

Attributes

Transformation

To have a transforming effect such as awe, wonderment (mysterium tremendum) or fascination (drawn to or transformative). To awaken one's deep emotions.
